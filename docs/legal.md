## Ethical Guidelines

### Responsible Testing
1. **Obtain written authorization** before testing systems you don't own
2. **Respect rate limits** and fair usage policies where possible
3. **Minimize impact** on target systems and users
4. **Report findings responsibly** to appropriate parties
5. **Follow disclosure policies** of target organizations

### Professional Standards
